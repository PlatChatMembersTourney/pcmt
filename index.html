What is the PCMT?

The Plat Chat Members Tournament (PCMT) is a community-run Valorant tournament organized by and for members of the Plat Chat community.

Whether you're here for your first taste of structured Valorant or just here for a good time, PCMT provides a competitive yet fun environment to test your skills and connect with other members.

How to Play

Ready to compete? Here's how to join PCMT:

  1. Become a YouTube Member - Support Plat Chat by becoming a YouTube Member to gain access to the community
  2. Join the Discord - Connect with other members and stay updated on tournament announcements

PCMT is an independent community project and is not officially affiliated with Plat Chat. If you're not already watching, check them out for the best Valorant esports content and analysis!
